AI Features and Data Processing

Gwenchana includes artificial intelligence (AI) features powered by OpenAI API. The AI tutor provides grammar explanations, translations, examples, and conversational feedback to help you improve your Korean language skills.

When you interact with AI features (for example, by asking questions in the grammar chat), your text input is securely transmitted to OpenAI's servers for processing. This allows the AI to generate personalized responses and explanations in your preferred language.

What data is shared: Only your text queries and the context necessary for generating relevant educational responses are shared with OpenAI.

What data is NOT shared: We do not share your personal identification data (such as email address, password, payment information, or account details) with OpenAI or any AI service providers.

AI interactions may be logged anonymously to improve service quality and accuracy. All processing is conducted in accordance with OpenAI's Privacy Policy and this Privacy Policy.

For detailed information about how AI features work and your responsibilities when using them, please review our Terms for using AI.
